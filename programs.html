Undergraduate Degrees

Bachelor of Science

BSc in Computer Science - Malawi

Computer science is a major source of technological innovation through the development of new software and computer applications, often leading to entrepreneurial activity. For this reason the Computer Science programme at Unicaf University incorporates modules on management, innovation and entrepreneurship, and an introduction to e-business. Graduates of the programme are therefore in a position to engage with the management and business side of the ICT sector, as well as the technical side of ICT, and have a starting point to launch their own business should they so wish.

The Unicaf University BSc programme in Computer Science consists of 20 modules, each contributing 24 credits to the final qualification. The programme structure and module composition is outlined in detail in section 4. The online nature of the programme allows it to be accessed by working people as well as full time students.


Graduate Degrees

Master of Science

MSc in Computer Science - Zambia

The aim of the programme is to generate computer professionals with scientific knowledge and skills essential for advancing their professional careers and/or proceed with further research and progress towards a doctoral degree.

The learning outcomes focus on developing a comprehensive understanding of modern networking and cybersecurity by equipping learners with the ability to identify physical and logical network controls, ensure reliable end-to-end data transmission, and recognize network attacks, malware, and defence mechanisms while applying appropriate countermeasures. They emphasize ethical practice in cybersecurity, the design and development of centralized and distributed systems, and the ability to analyze, detect, respond to, and protect against unauthorized or malicious activities in computer networks. Learners are also expected to implement intelligence collection through networks, evaluate cyber-related decisions within national security contexts from social, ethical, legal, and political perspectives, and understand common web and application attack vectors. Additionally, the outcomes include designing enterprise-wide information security infrastructures, developing secure web database applications, and communicating technical and analytical ideas clearly and effectively in professional and academic settings.


Master of Science

MSc in Web Design and Development - Zambia

The programme aims to equip students with professional-level competence in web design and content management by providing hands-on experience in creating and evaluating live websites, while fostering familiarity with the rapidly evolving body of knowledge in the web design discipline. Its objectives focus on developing successful and excellent web designers and developers by applying theoretical principles to real-life practice and preparing students to adapt and thrive as professionals in the dynamic web design and development industry.

During this course, students will be able to study basic principles within the Web Design world and become fully equipped with all the necessary skills and knowledge so as to become successful web designers and developers.
